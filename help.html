SPECIAL KEYS
Page Up - Show next page of carousel
Page Down - Show previous page of carousel
F11 - Hide/Reveal
CTRL-Z - Undo
Alt-Gr - Show control codes
TEXT COLOURS
A Shift-F1 - Red text
A Shift-F2 - Green text
A Shift-F3 - Yellow text
A Shift-F4 - Blue text
A Shift-F5 - Magenta text
A Shift-F6 - Cyan text
A Shift-F7 - White text
A Shift-F8 - Black text
